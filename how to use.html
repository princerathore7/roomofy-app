How to Use Roomofy

  1. Signup: Create your account by entering your mobile number and password.
  2. Login: Enter your registered details to access the dashboard.
  3. Browse Rooms: See available rooms with details like price, location, and facilities.
  4. View Photos: Click on any room photo to see it in full-screen view.
  5. Contact Owner: Use the WhatsApp/Call button to directly connect with the room owner.
  6. Post a Room: If you are an ownerof an hotel, contact the admin panel to list your room with photos and price.
