About Us

Welcome to Roomofy – your trusted partner for finding the perfect room with ease. Our mission is to connect room owners with tenants in the simplest, most transparent, and reliable way possible.

Whether you are a student, a working professional, or a family, Roomofy helps you find budget-friendly and comfortable rooms at your desired location. Owners can list their rooms hassle-free, and users can directly connect with them.

We believe in simplicity, trust, and accessibility. That’s why Roomofy is designed to be easy to use, fast, and secure.
